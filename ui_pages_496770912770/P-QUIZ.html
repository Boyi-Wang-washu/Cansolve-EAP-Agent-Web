1. Choose the correct past tense form:

She ______ to the store yesterday.

A go
B goes
C went
D gone

Correct Answer: C) went

"Went" is the simple past tense of "go". The sentence refers to a completed action in the past ("yesterday"), so we use simple past tense.

2. Fill in the blank with the correct past tense verb:

They ______ dinner together last night.

3. Identify and correct the grammar error:

Incorrect: I seed the movie last weekend.

4. Short Answer Question:

Explain the difference between "I have eaten" and "I ate" in terms of tense and usage.

Model Answer:

"I have eaten" is present perfect tense, used to describe an action that happened at an unspecified time before now or has a connection to the present. "I ate" is simple past tense, used for a completed action at a specific time in the past.
